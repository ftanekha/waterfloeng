A little word about Us

Waterflo Engineering was founded in the late '60s, and has always focused highly on providing irrigation and water engineering solutions. Our personnel comprises of expert professionals in the field. Currently our company has 2 branches, for further info please refer to our contact details section.

About our products

We manufacture a range of products, mainly alluminium irrigation fittings e.g. priming pumps & pipes. Our products also include pumps sets, valves, tanks & solar panels, and reservoirs (Note: this includes design & construction).

Meet the team

Our company employs a substantial number of skilled workers with globally recognized qualifications. Our staff members come from all walks of life. We work together with a simple moto in mind: 'Moving water is our passion, Irrigation is our business !'
